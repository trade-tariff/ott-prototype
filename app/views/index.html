Trade Tariff: look up commodity codes, duty and VAT rates

This service will help you to:

  • identify the correct commodity code for your goods
  • check if you need a licence or other documentation to move your goods
  • make sure you pay the right Customs Duty and import VAT
  • complete import or export declarations and other paperwork
  • understand rules of origin for the UK's trade agreements

It will also help you check if your goods are covered by:

  • Agricultural Policy
  • anti-dumping duties
  • UK safeguarding measures
  • tariff quotas

What are commodity codes?

Commodity codes are 10-digit numbers that are used to identify and classify goods you want to import or export, so you can fill in declarations and other paperwork. You must use the right commodity code in customs declarations.
