UK Goods classification 2021 to 2022 correlation table

  • From 1 January 2022, the UK will introduce its 2022 Integrated Tariff. This will incorporate the WCO's changes to the Harmonised System Nomenclature. View the correlation of commodity codes, at 8-digit level, from the 2021 Tariff to that of 2022.
