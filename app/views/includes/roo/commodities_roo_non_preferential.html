Non-preferential rules of origin

The Customs (Origin of Chargeable Goods) (EU Exit) Regulations 2020 (opens in new tab) establishes the UK's rules of origin regime that applies to imports from countries which the UK does not have a preferential trading arrangement.

Non-preferential rules of origin allows the implementation of several commercial policy measures such as:

  • anti-dumping duties
  • countervailing duties
  • trade embargoes
  • safeguarding measures
  • quantitative restrictions
  • tariff quotas

They are also used for trade statistics, public tenders and origin marking.
