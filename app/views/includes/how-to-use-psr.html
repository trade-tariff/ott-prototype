The rules of origin table contains:

  • the product classification heading ('HS heading') or chapter number (column 1)
  • a description of the product, or the headings / subheadings covered by the rules (column 2)
  • a description of how non-originating materials must be worked or processed to get country of origin status (column 3)

For each row:

  • check column 1 to find the product classification heading or chapter number for the product being exported
  • check column 2 to find the description that applies to the product
  • check column 3 to see how the product must be worked or processed to gain country of origin status

Unless preceded by ‘ex’, the rules in column 3 apply to all products that come under the headings and chapters in column 1.

If the heading in column 1 starts with ‘ex’, the rules in column 3 will only apply to the product as described in column 2.
