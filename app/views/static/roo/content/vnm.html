"VNM" means the value of non-originating materials used in the manufacture of the product which is its customs value at the time of importation including freight, insurance where appropriate, packing and all the other costs incurred in transporting the materials to the importation port in the Party where the producer of the good is located.

Where it is not known and cannot be ascertained, the first ascertainable price paid for the non-originating materials is used.
